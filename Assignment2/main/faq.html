Frequently Asked Questions

General

What is the Plot Twist?

Plot Twist is an online story planner that provides you with the tools you need to plan, organize and create stories, fictional worlds, characters and more.

Is Plot Twist free to use?

Plot Twist runs as a freemium app. You get all the necessities for writing for free, and the nice-to-have features with a paid subscription.

Who else can see my work?

Only you can see your work unless you explicitly share your work with our sharing feature.

Can I write collaboratively with others?

This is a feature we are looking to add very soon.

Do I own the copyright to content I upload?

You own all the rights to everything you write. We reserve the right to store it on our servers.

How can I get involved in the Plot Twist project?

You can get involved in the Plot Twist project by visiting our GitHub repository and contributing to the codebase.
